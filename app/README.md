This is an AI Curriculum Generator that will bring together human-written resources from the Internet to create a full roadmap to learning a new subject or skill. The AI doesn't generate any content, but compiles human resources together with a human-written curriculum. 

Progress:
Nowhere close to being finished, we have implemented the resource finding AI and created one curriculum which is under review. Steps to do are to allow users to generate their own curriculum rather than curricula being carefully checked by us.
